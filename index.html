Amazing Ocean Facts

Ocean Depths

The average depth of the ocean is about 12,100 feet. The deepest part is called the Challenger Deep in the Mariana Trench.

Marine Biodiversity

Scientists estimate that about one million species of animals live in the ocean, with 95% being invertebrates.

Coral Reefs

Coral reefs cover less than 1% of the ocean floor, yet they support about 25% of all marine creatures.

Ocean Currents

Ocean currents are like giant conveyor belts moving water and heat around the globe, influencing our climate.
